Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

Introduction

Hi1 Labs ("we", "our", or "us") operates the MyApp mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Our app is designed with a privacy-first approach — we collect only what is strictly necessary for the App to function.

Information We Collect

We believe in minimal data collection. Here is a summary of the data our App handles:

Data Type Collected? Purpose Shared?
Crash logs Yes App stability & bug fixing Firebase Crashlytics
Anonymous usage analytics Yes Improve app features Firebase Analytics
User-created content Stored locally only Core app functionality No
Personal information (name, email) No — No
Location data No — No

Third-Party Services

Our App may use the following third-party services that have their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase Analytics — anonymous usage statistics
  • Firebase Crashlytics — crash reporting

Data Storage & Security

All user-created content is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. We do not maintain any external database of user data.

Anonymous analytics data processed by third-party services (Firebase) is handled according to their respective privacy policies and data processing agreements.

Data Retention & Deletion

Since user data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the App or clearing the App's data through your device settings.

Anonymous analytics data is retained for up to 14 months by Firebase Analytics, after which it is automatically deleted.

Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately at privacy@hi1labs.com.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your data, including the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. Since we collect minimal data and store user content only locally on your device, most data management is directly in your control.

For any data-related requests, please contact us at privacy@hi1labs.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
