About

Built by one, for everyone

Hi1 Labs is a one-person independent app studio. Every app is designed, developed, and maintained by a single developer who cares deeply about the user experience.

Built with Flutter for both iOS and Android, every app ships with clean design, smooth performance, and respect for your privacy. No tracking beyond what's necessary, no selling your data, no dark patterns.

Have an idea for an app, or feedback on an existing one? I'd love to hear from you.
